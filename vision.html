    VISION

    Everything starts with a vision. Positive change needs a clear goal to work toward. Make it happen.

Our vision is to help shape and inspire through visual art how people and organisations understand, engage and recognised home for powerful conflict art and artists.

  • We showcase conflict art

    As a profound communicative tool to understand the complex issues of conflict.

  • We influence stakeholders

    Decision makers, NGOs, the public and art critics.

  • We build relationships and communities

    By recognising artists and collaborating with organisations.
