The exhibition showing the award winners and the shortlisted artists will take place at Spazju Kreattiv in Valetta, 3-26th of March, 2017. It has been endorsed by Valetta 2018, and as part of a partnership between the Artraker Foundation and British Council (Malta), the event also benefits from the multilevel support of Spazju Kreattiv, the Malta Arts Council and the Ministry of Education. It will also mark the celebration of the British Council (Malta)'s 80th Anniversary.
